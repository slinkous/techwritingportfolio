Adding an activity

  1. From the home menu, tap the icon and select "Add activity".

  2. Use the icon to access drop-down options for the activity type, date, start and end times.

  3. Customize optional fields: a. If you would like to your activity a title or notes, tap the "Activity title" or "Notes" boxes to active the on-screen keyboard. b. To change calories, steps, or miles, tap the box you want to change to open the on-screen number pad.

    Note: Move Minutes and Heart Points are only calculated by Fit and cannot be adjusted

  4. Tap "Save" in the top right corner.

Further Information: Available fields for activity recording, Manual activity tracking
