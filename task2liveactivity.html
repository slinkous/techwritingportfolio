Tracking a live activity

Start a workout

  1. From the home menu, tap the icon and select "Track workout".

  2. Tap the icon to access drop-down options for the activity type.

    Note: To accurately track your route in an activity which saves map position, make sure the GPS is enabled on your Android device.

  3. Tap "Start workout"

Pause and stop a workout

  1. Once a workout has started, press Pause to stop the timer.
  2. To resume the workout, press Play.
  3. At the end of the workout, press Stop.
